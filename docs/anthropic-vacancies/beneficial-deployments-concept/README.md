# beneficial-deployments-concept/ — concept document outline для Anthropic outreach

В диалоге Claude разработал outline профессионального **concept brief** (8–15 страниц) для Anthropic Beneficial Deployments team. Это конкретный application пути «Anthropic Institute → Beneficial Deployments → Applied AI Engineer» из [`profile-mapping/03-integral-final/02-final-ranking.md`](../profile-mapping/03-integral-final/02-final-ranking.md).

Concept document отвечает на 8 ключевых вопросов:

1. What is the problem? Specifically.
2. Who is affected? Concretely, with numbers.
3. What is the proposed solution? Architecture-level.
4. Why now? What changed that makes this possible.
5. What's the role of Anthropic? Specifically.
6. What's the role of you (the proposer)? Specifically.
7. How would success be measured? Concrete metrics.
8. What are the risks? Honestly. Why should they engage?

## Ключевые ограничения

- **NOT a grant application** — менее формально, без конкретной денежной суммы.
- **NOT an academic paper** — без extensive literature review.
- **NOT a business plan** — без детальных финансовых проекций.
- **NOT a theoretical document** — фокус на actionable deployment.
- **NOT advocacy** — представляет возможность, не требует action.

## Pilot scope (Section 4)

- **Initial domain**: Persönliches Budget cases under § 78 SGB IX
- **Phase 0** (months 1–3): Personal-tool building, single advocate
- **Phase 1** (months 4–9): Limited collaboration, 2–5 advocates
- **Phase 2** (далее): scale на основе результатов
