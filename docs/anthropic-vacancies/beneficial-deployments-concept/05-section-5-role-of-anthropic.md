Section 5: Role of Anthropic Beneficial Deployments

What we'd ask:

Claude API access at sustainable cost (perhaps subsidized для beneficial domain)

Cowork access for advocates' UX layer

Technical guidance on best practices for vulnerable populations applications

Research partnership — это actually case study for Anthropic's understanding how Claude serves в high-stakes legal/administrative contexts

Possible foundation funding through their grant programs (если applicable)

What Anthropic gains:

Real-world deployment data — how Claude performs serving vulnerable populations

Case study для Beneficial Deployments program

Validation of Claude's capability в German legal domain

Demonstration что AI serves social welfare effectively

Research insights на best practices for similar future deployments

This is mutual benefit framing. Не «вы дайте денег», но «давайте collaborate on something both parties value».
