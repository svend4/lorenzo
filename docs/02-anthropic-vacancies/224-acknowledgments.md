## Acknowledgments

This paper emerged through dialogue with Claude (Anthropic)
on April 26, 2026, prompted by author's review of the
Russian-language interview with Konstantin Chukavin in
Fontanka.ru describing «Обучай». The fivefold typology
of principal-side agents emerged from the user's observation
that "Representative Agent" was an insufficient category
to capture the full landscape.

Konstantin Chukavin's «Обучай» service serves as central
empirical reference. Public information from the Fontanka
interview informs case study. No formal partnership with
or endorsement of «Обучай» exists; it is documented as
publicly observable case.

The author retained editorial authority. All claims and
errors are author's responsibility.
